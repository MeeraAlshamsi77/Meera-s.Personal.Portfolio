Meera's Digital Scrapbook

A small, organized corner of the internet where four interactive projects sit together like pages in a scrapbook.

Ideas Made Interactive Code With Character Digital Storytelling

Hi, I'm Meera. A freshmen at NYU Abu Dhabi. I like when websites feel a bit like someone's personal scrapbook: taped edges, soft colors, and just enough structure so you don't get lost.

This portfolio collects four projects I worked on this semester in Communications Lab, each one exploring a different way to share an interactive story:

  • a panicky sprint through running late to class
  • a dreamy space where you blur the line between drawing and dreaming
  • a sound-led murder mystery that you solve with what you hear
  • a loop on the “fifth floor” that cant be escaped
what this site is
One desk, four pages.
Think of this as a neat desk, not a messy drawer: one cover spread one shelf of projects, and one place to say hi. Everything is meant to feel layered and handmade, and a journey to flip through.
How to explore
1. Click My Projects to see the four project cards.
2. Hover and peek at each faded live preview.
3. Click a card (or use Surprise Me) to open a project in the scrapbook.
4. Head to Get in Touch if you want to share thoughts or questions.
